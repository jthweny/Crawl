Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Crawl (“we”, “us”) is an iOS app that builds small, live apps from your prompts. This policy explains what data we handle, why, and what control you have. The short version: we store only what the product needs to work, we don’t run ads, we don’t track you across apps, and we never sell your data.

What we collect

Account. You sign in with Apple. We receive a unique user identifier and your email address (which may be Apple’s private relay address if you chose “Hide My Email”). You also give the app a display name, used only to greet you.

Your content. Your chats with Crawl, the apps it builds for you, and any preferences or custom instructions you save. These are stored on our servers so your work follows your account.

Credit and purchases. Your credit balance, a usage ledger of what each AI request cost, and records of credit top-ups you buy. Payments are processed entirely by Apple — we never see your payment details, only Apple’s transaction identifier and the product purchased.

What we don’t collect. No advertising identifiers, no analytics profiles, no contacts, no browsing history.

Device features (camera, microphone, location)

Apps you build in Crawl can ask to use your camera, your location, or your microphone’s loudness level. Each use is gated behind the standard iOS permission prompt (plus an extra in-app consent for apps that came from a share link). The data is handed to the app you built on your device. It is not stored on our servers. The microphone skill senses loudness only — audio is never recorded or transmitted.

AI processing

Your prompts and the app being edited are sent to our servers and relayed to an AI model provider (via OpenRouter) to generate a response. This processing is ephemeral: providers act as processors to service the request. We do not use your content to train models.

Sharing

If you create a share link for a chat, a snapshot of that chat (and its app) becomes retrievable by anyone with the link, and recipients can fork a private copy into their own account. You can revoke a share link at any time; revoking stops new access, but copies already forked belong to their recipients. Sharing is entirely optional — nothing is shared unless you create a link.

Service providers

  • Apple — sign-in and payment processing.
  • Supabase — database and backend hosting (account, content, credit data).
  • OpenRouter — AI model routing (prompt and app content, ephemerally).
  • Public data APIs (weather, dictionaries, and similar) — receive only the query parameters the app you built sends (for example, coordinates for a weather lookup), never your identity.

Retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account exists. You can delete your account at any time in the app: Settings → Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, profile, chats, built apps, share links, usage history, and any remaining credit. Purchase records are kept in anonymised form (no longer linked to you) for fraud prevention and financial record-keeping.

Security

Data travels over TLS. On device, session tokens live in the iOS Keychain. Server-side, per-user row-level security isolates each account’s data, and AI provider keys never ship inside the app.

Children

Crawl is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we’ll update this page and the effective date, and note the change in the app or release notes.
